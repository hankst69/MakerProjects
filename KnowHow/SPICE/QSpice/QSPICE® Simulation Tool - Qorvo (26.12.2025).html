QSPICE® Simulator

The next generation of SPICE is here. Qorvo’s QSPICE® simulator for analog and mixed-signal systems gives circuit designers the ability to evaluate their designs with complete confidence in the results.

Free for all to use and standardize upon, QSPICE delivers significantly better SPICE basics, supports vast amounts of digital logic without performance penalties, and provides unprecedented speed and accuracy. Its intuitive interface features publication-quality graphics and powerful built-in tools, including model generator functionality for creating device models in minutes and Python integration for automating simulation and analysis.

Benefits

  • Better SPICE basics: higher simulation speed, functionality, accuracy, and reliability
  • Supports massive amounts of digital
  • Free for commercial use
  • Create MOSFET, diode, and JFET models in minutes
  • C++/Verilog compilers included
  • Publication-quality graphics

System Requirements

  • Windows 11 or a 64-bit edition of Windows 10
  • 4 GB RAM minimum (16 GB recommended)
  • 100 MB disk space for software installation
  • At least 16 GB disk space for simulation data
